# Contribution Guidelines

- Contributions to repositories should come from projects that are actively
  maintained and properly documented.
- Contributions in the form of blog posts, videos, or podcasts should
  be relevant to the project.
- All provided links must be functional and up to date.

## Contributing

1. [Fork the repo.](https://github.com/kondratevdev/awesome-django-modern-rest/fork)
2. Create a new branch for your changes.
3. Add new items to the awesome list.
4. Commit your changes.
5. Push the branch to your fork and open a pull request.
6. Give yourself a pat on the back and wait for your pull request
  to be reviewed and merged.
